Fishing Tutorial Guide:
Would you like to learn more about Fish Swarms? Open the map (Alt + M) to see them: they are marked with a fish symbol. Fish is not found everywhere, so tracking it on the map is essential.
And even if you don't have necessary equipment in your hands, you will be automatically offered to fish when you are near a Fish Swarm.
Remember to always have your bait, fishing rod and float ready.
Remember where you can go fishing: Fellmere Lake Vicinity, Wasterland South Coast, Langk Lizardmen Dwellings Coast, Floran Village Vicinity, Plains of the Lizardmen Southern Reservoirs, Ivory Tower Western Resevoirs, Narsell Lake. Besides, you can fish in safe areas, such as Gludin Harbor, Giran Harbor and to the east of Giran, as well as nearby the store in Aden.
