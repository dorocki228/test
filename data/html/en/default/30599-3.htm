Newbie Guide:
Most likely, you got two types of weapons. A dagger and one academic knightly sword. You probably already noticed how much they differ from each other. Dagger is fast, but it has a low accuracy, Training Sword Knight (one-handed sword) slower, but has a high accuracy. Use the weapons, which is more suitable for you. When will type 5000 Aden, you can change the dagger on a Bone Dagger and a sword in the Short Sword.
Armor is usually mined in battles with monsters, but if to achieve level 5 you will not find anything, you can buy them in the shop.
