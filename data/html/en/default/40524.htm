Hello, Warrior! With my help, you can visit the Labyrinth of the Abyss with your friends and get a lot of fun and valuable items!
To enter the instance, you need:
- A group of people from 3+ people; - 1 Piece of Glory World for each participant   or Club Card.
Instance zone available once per day. Cooldown on entry is reset automatically every day at 6:30 (GMT+3).
You can check the availability of the instance using command /instancezone
