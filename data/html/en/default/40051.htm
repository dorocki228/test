Castle Siege
Basic:
Sieges of castles are held every day, alternating castles at 20:00 MSK. Registration is available from level 62. The award for the siege of the castles is received by the faction that seized the castle.
Siege process:
The duration of the siege is 1 hour. The winner is the one who, at the end of time, owns the castle. To obtain the ability to capture the castle - you need to destroy all the crystals.
What does the winner / loser receive ?:
The faction that captured the enemy castle receives a reward in the form of 300 Adena and unique passive effects. The faction that owns a large number of castles receives a double advantage in the time of revival on the daily epic bosses. The fraction that could not keep its castle, in the city appears a 15% tax on trade through a privat store. The tax disappears as soon as the faction regains the castle.
