Fortresses:
In the game world, there are three fortresses that can be captured, namely:
Northern fortress Western Fortress Eastern Fortress
To begin the siege of the fortress, you just need to approach it and kill 1 guard.
The siege can not begin earlier than 2 hours before the field of the previous siege of this fortress.
For the killing of guards and capture - all players receive valuable rewards.
If the clan takes possession of the fortress - all its members receive a passive skill that gives an addition to the characteristics and also every 30 minutes, the clan gets a certain amount of clan reputation.
