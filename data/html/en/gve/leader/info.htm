The Faction Leader is the most worthy player who was elected to this position by voting.
The Leader has the right to use a special global chat and leader commands..
In addition, the Leader receives a set of additional skills that will help him and other members of the faction during the battles.

Leader Aura Increase P.Def and M.Def by 10%, Speed by 5 of the nearest members of the faction.

Leader Fortitude Passively increase P.Def and M.Def of the Leader by 35%.

Faction Invincibility Makes nearby faction members invincible for 30 seconds.
